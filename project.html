FAQ

Frequently asked questions:

What is the purpose of your website? — The purpose of our website is to provide you with rapid conversion of units.

What is conversion of units and how does it work? — Conversion of units is the conversion between different units of measurement for the same quantity, typically through multiplicative conversion factors.

Which converters do you provide? — Actually, we can provide you with 7 converters: weight and mass conversion, length and distance conversion, volume conversion, digital data conversion, speed conversion, area conversion and the last - temperature conversion.

Do I have to pay to use your site's services? — No, you don't have to pay money for using our services, our site works for free for all users.

What should I do if I notice an error or a bug? — If you've spotted an error or bug, please, feel free to email us (unitconverter-fb@gmail.com) and describe your issue.
